LiFi

Introducing LiFi, a revolutionary wireless communication technology that stands for 'Light Fidelity'. Unlike WiFi, which uses radio waves, LiFi utilises light waves to transmit internet data.

The purpose of this website is to explore LiFi, a new and emerging technology which could restructure the internet.

My Name is Yannick Hott. I am a Year 9 student at Waverley College. I have a firm interest in technological development, and LiFi is one area which I am passionate about. I study IST, a subject revolving around modern technology, and have been firmly invested in gaining more knowledge about the technological world.

LiFi is a system which transmits data through illumination (LED bulbs). In the 1990's, it was discovered that LED's could transmit data, and Professor Harald Hass demonstrated this in a 2011 TEDGlobal talk in Edinburgh.
